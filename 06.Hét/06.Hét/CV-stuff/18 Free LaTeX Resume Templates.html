The 18 Best LaTeX Resume Templates

Creating a LaTeX resume template from scratch is no easy task. Why do the hard work when there are plenty of free LaTeX resumes available online? We’ve rounded up the best LaTeX resume templates for you to choose from.

LaTeX is one of the most powerful word-processing tools available for creating resume templates, but it’s also one of the most complex.

It takes significantly longer to create a resume or CV in LaTeX compared to Microsoft Word or Google Docs. If you don’t know how to use the editor, your best option for creating a LaTeX resume is to download a free resume template.

We’ve scoured the web to provide you with the best LaTeX resume templates that are free to download and easy to edit.

Our free-to-use resume builder can make you a resume in as little as 5 minutes. Just pick the template you want, and our software will format everything for you.

Resume Genius LaTeX resume templates

Our LaTeX templates offer a sleek, modern look while allowing you to easily customize the content and formatting.

To use our templates, download the source files and either upload them or paste their contents into your favorite LaTeX editor.

1. Chicago

Whether you’re a seasoned professional or a just starting your career, this straightforward design ensures your qualifications stand out without any unnecessary frills.

2. Milano

This template strikes an excellent balance between visual appeal and functionality. Its polished, minimalist design conveys professionalism while ensuring that the content remains the focus.

3. Classic

Our Classic template lets you go into detail about your skills and achievements while maintaining a clean, modern look. It’s the kind of template that would work well for any professional field.

4. Easy

This template’s layout is simple and intuitive. It uses a single-column format that’s easy to scan. The balance between text and white space gives the document a polished, uncluttered appearance while leaving plenty of room to elaborate on your accomplishments.

5. Empire State

The Empire State template features a minimalist aesthetic, focusing on clarity and efficient use of space. The black-and-white color scheme and clean layout create a bold, no-nonsense impression right from the start.

Other LaTeX resume templates

Whether you’re in tech, business, or a creative field, these templates offer professional designs to showcase your unique skills and experiences.

1. Contemporary

Need a clear and professional LaTeX resume template? This one’s for you. Each section is highlighted in red, and the resume’s margins and lines create just the right amount of white space.

2. Cutting-edge

If you’d like a modern LaTeX resume template, check out this one provided by Overleaf. You can add a personalized QR code with a logo that links to your online portfolio, LinkedIn profile, or personal website.

If you’re applying for a job in the US, don’t include a photo on your resume.

3. Innovative

This downloadable resume template from ShareLaTeX provides plenty of space for your work experience. It’s a great option if you’ve had a long and distinguished career.

4. Project highlights

This efficient resume template allows you to focus on your work experience while also showcasing particular programming projects that show how you’ve honed your unique skill set.

5. Software engineer

This resume template includes multiple skills sections, making it easy to separate your hard skills from your soft skills. The large empty section on the left-hand side can be used however you like — for example, to add your certifications or relevant coursework.

6. Engineering

A formal design featuring a space to list your GitHub, this template is made for engineers. Not only that, but this template includes a large work experience section, giving you room to summarize all your professional achievements.

7. Technical

With a unique sidebar and compact layout, this LaTeX resume template is perfect for candidates writing a technical resume.

The sidebar gives you plenty of space for a detailed skills section, where you can list all the programming languages and tools you’re experienced with.

Additionally, this template provides you with room to expand on various projects on your resume, allowing you to show off some of your biggest achievements.

8. Academic

Do you need a resume that shows off your academic accomplishments? This template is perfect for you. Featuring a large education section and space for any academic or personal projects, this template makes it easy to highlight all your skills and qualifications.

9. Simple

Want a formal, basic resume? This simple LaTeX resume template will help you highlight your qualifications with a sleek, minimal design.

The template’s dense sections and simple headers give you maximum space to talk about your professional accomplishments. And with its formal design, it won’t ever attract attention for the wrong reasons.

10. Scholarly

This template is perfect if you’re an academic with minimal work experience. It features all the key sections your resume needs, including publications, relevant coursework, research, and of course — your education section. All while remaining one page long.

11. Unique

This single-page template lets you elaborate on your academic achievements and expand on your professional experience.

Light shading separates the skills section, language proficiency, and contact details from the two biggest sections of your CV — your work experience and academic accomplishments.

12. Creative

With its bright red colors and bold headers, this template is a great choice if you want to show off your creative side. For example, if you’re a front end developer or UX designer, this is a great template for you because it helps you stand out from other applicants and shows off your design sensibilities.

13. Europass

Looking for work in the EU? This neatly organized Europass template gives you space to list all your relevant qualifications in a Europass-compliant format.
